Teso North Constituency

Teso North Constituency is more than just a geographical location; it's a vibrant community of individuals striving for progress and prosperity. Led by our esteemed Member of Parliament, Hon. Oku Kaunya, we are committed to fostering positive change and ensuring the welfare of every citizen.

Mission

To serve as a bridge between the aspirations of our constituents and the actions of our government. We aim to facilitate effective communication, promote transparency, and implement initiatives that address the needs of our community comprehensively.

Vision

Inclusive growth, where every individual has the opportunity to thrive and contribute meaningfully to the collective prosperity of our constituency.

Online Services

At Teso North, we believe in creating opportunities for all members of our community to thrive and succeed. Through various programs and initiatives, we aim to empower individuals from diverse backgrounds and fields of study. Whether it's providing scholarships, bursaries, or job opportunities, our goal is to support the aspirations and dreams of our beneficiaries, ensuring they have the resources and encouragement needed to reach their full potential.
