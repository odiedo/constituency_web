Headlines: Teso North Constituency is situated in the western part of Kenya, within Busia County. The constituency is currently represented by Hon. Oku Kaunya. The constituency covers an area of approximately 257.10 square kilometers with a population of 117,974 people. Population: The population of Teso North Constituency is diverse, with residents from various ethnic backgrounds contributing to the cultural richness of the area. economic activities: in Teso North are diverse, including agriculture, trade, and other forms of commerce. Agriculture is often a significant contributor to the local economy, with residents engaged in farming activities.
